Feed-Forward Networks

Expanding and Compressing Semantic Space

After Attention figures out how words relate to each other, the Feed-Forward Network (FFN) processes each word individually. It projects the vector into a massive hidden dimensional space to "memorize" concepts and untangle complex boundaries, then compresses it back down.
