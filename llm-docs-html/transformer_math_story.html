This is exactly how a transformer updates values! It takes the new Contextual Information generated by the Attention mechanism and physically adds it back onto the Original Word Embedding vector.

Look at the numbers: 1.0 changed to 1.294. The word "AI" has literally, mathematically evolved to contain the context of "learns". Because of this Residual Addition, gradients easily flow untouched during Backpropagation, preventing vanishing gradients.
