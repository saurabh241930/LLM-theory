The Alignment Problem

Pre-trained models generate text based on training data. They aren't optimized to be helpful, harmless, or honest. They might hallucinate or refuse to help.

Solution: Alignment (Instruct Tuning, RLHF, DPO) teaches the model to follow human preferences.

Instruction Tuning

Teach the model to follow instructions by training on datasets of {Instruction, Output} pairs (e.g., Alpaca, ShareGPT).

  • Datasets: Alpaca (52k), ShareGPT (500k).
  • Result: Model learns "If prompt X, then generate Y".

DPO (Direct Preference Optimization)

DPO is a modern, simpler alternative to RLHF. It skips the explicit reward model and directly optimizes for human preferences in one step.

  • RLHF: 3 stages. Complex. 2-4 weeks. $100K+.
  • DPO: 1 stage (direct fine-tune on preferences). Days. $10K.

Key Takeaways

✅Instruct Tuning: Teaches model to follow instructions (~70-80% quality).
✅RLHF: Uses human ranking + RL to optimize preferences (~95% quality). Expensive ($100K+).
✅DPO: Simplifies RLHF. Direct preference fine-tuning. Days vs. 10x cheaper.
✅Quality: Progression: Pre-trained (50%) → Instruct (75%) → RLHF/DPO (90%+).
